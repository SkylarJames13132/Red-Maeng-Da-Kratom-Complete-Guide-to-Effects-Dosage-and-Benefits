## Storage and Shelf Life of Red Maeng Da Kratom

Proper storage maintains potency and prevents degradation of red Maeng Da kratom over time.

### Optimal Storage Conditions

**Temperature**: Store in a cool, dry place away from heat sources. Ideal temperature range is 60-75¬∞F (15-24¬∞C).

**Light Protection**: Keep kratom away from direct sunlight, which can degrade alkaloids. Dark containers or storage areas work best.

**Moisture Control**: Humidity is kratom's enemy. Use airtight containers and consider silica gel packets in humid climates.

**Container Selection**: Glass jars with tight seals, vacuum-sealed bags, or food-grade plastic containers with secure lids all work well.

### Shelf Life Expectations

When stored properly:

- **Powder form**: Maintains potency for 1-3 months at peak freshness, remains usable for up to 12 months
- **Capsules**: Similar to powder, though gelatin capsules may degrade slightly faster in humid conditions
- **Vacuum-sealed packages**: Can extend shelf life to 12-18 months or longer

### Signs of Degradation

Watch for these indicators that kratom has lost potency or gone bad:

- Significant color change (fading or darkening)
- Musty or unusual odor
- Clumping or moisture presence
- Reduced effects at normal doses
- Visible mold growth (discard immediately)
